The Context-Aware User Interface guides caregivers and care recipients through the process of customizing assistive devices based on individual needs and care contexts. By capturing user abilities, caregiving goals, and environmental constraints, it generates tailored module recommendations and explains the rationale behind each suggestion. The interface makes technical trade-offs transparent and supports informed decision-making without requiring engineering expertise. It also provides step-by-step guidance for configuration and deployment, enabling accessible and structured personalization.
